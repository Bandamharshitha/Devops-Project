Our Mission to Save Lives

LifeStream is dedicated to creating a seamless connection between blood donors and those in need, ensuring timely access to life-saving blood across India.

Our Mission & Vision

Save Lives

To ensure that no one dies due to unavailability of blood when every second counts.

Build Community

Create a nationwide network of voluntary blood donors ready to help in emergencies.

Instant Response

Leverage technology to provide real-time blood availability and rapid matching.

Our Story

LifeStream was founded in 2018 after our founder, Dr. Arjun Mehta, witnessed a tragic incident where a young accident victim couldn't get the required blood type in time. This preventable loss inspired the creation of a platform that could connect blood donors with recipients in real-time.

What started as a small initiative in Bangalore has now grown into a nationwide network with over 350 partner hospitals and thousands of dedicated donors. Our technology has been responsible for matching over 23,450 blood units to those in need, saving countless lives across India.

Today, LifeStream continues to innovate with predictive analytics for blood shortage forecasting, mobile blood donation camps, and educational programs to encourage regular voluntary donation.
